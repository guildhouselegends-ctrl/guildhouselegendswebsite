Meet Your Dungeon Masters

Mason
10 Years of Dungeon Master Experience

My name is Mason and I'm one of the Dungeon Masters running games on this server. Dungeons and Dragons has always been a great way for me to feed my nerdy tendencies while engaging in a social activity that feels fun and encouraging. I hope that with 10 years of Dungeon Master experience, I can bring the same experience to the players here at Guildhouse Legends. I am always trying to find new ideas and stories to explore within the main core pillars of DnD: Exploration, Roleplay, and Combat. I always strive to create engaging NPCs, settings full of depth and character, and adventures that push the bounds of your creativity and curiosity. I enjoy facilitating these types of cooperative games and look forward to seeing new players at the table each week!

Slater
7 Years · Professional Entertainer & Thespian

I'm Slater — a long time gamer, professional entertainer, and thespian at heart. For the past 7 years, I've worked in the entertainment industry while playing and facilitating Dungeons & Dragons. My Dungeon Master style is rooted in theater and I endeavor to bring that approach to all of my stories and in-game characters. I focus on performance, character, and imagination above all else. Players in the games I run should feel immersed in the fantasy world we create together, and free to express themselves through their character's eyes. Maps, music, and dice are tools we use to bring our imaginations into the game. I look forward to the situations and stories we'll face together!
